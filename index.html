Our riders deliver verified, community-powered mobility at scale. For every trip completed, ORDA RIDES ensures real-time safety checks, transparent pricing, and same-day payouts. That's 3 times more secure and 2 times more reliable than informal okada services—without sacrificing speed or local knowledge.

Ride for Us

By enlisting local businesses and market vendors, our platform strengthens community economies while streamlining logistics. ORDA RIDES enables merchants to schedule deliveries, reach new customers, and reduce transport delays by up to 60% compared to informal courier options. Every transaction supports local growth—keeping revenue, trust, and opportunity circulating locally.

Enlist Your Business

The founding team—all born and raised in the neighborhoods we serve—designed ORDA RIDES to run on low-bandwidth networks and affordable devices. That means 4× faster load times and 80% lower data usage than conventional ride-hailing platforms. Every line of code reflects the realities of Ghanaian streets—and the dreams of those who walk them.
