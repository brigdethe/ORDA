FAQs

Use the ORDA app, USSD, or SMS.

  1. App: Open the app → enter pickup and destination → choose vehicle → confirm price → request.
  2. USSD: Dial the ORDA short code → follow prompts to share pickup/destination → confirm request.
  3. SMS: Text your pickup and destination in the format “Ride: From [pickup] to [destination]” → reply to confirm.

Choose based on your trip:

  • Motorbike: Fastest for one passenger and small parcels.
  • Tricycle: Great for short trips and medium cargo.
  • Car: Comfortable rides, families, and weather protection.
